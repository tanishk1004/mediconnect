## 🚨 The Problem

**3.5 billion people** lack access to basic healthcare. Rural patients face:

- **60+ km travel** to reach the nearest hospital
- **70% can't afford** travel costs + consultation fees
- **1 doctor per 10,000 people** in rural areas (vs 1:300 in cities)
- **3x higher maternal mortality** due to lack of prenatal care
- **80% late-stage diagnosis** of chronic diseases like diabetes and cancer

**Result:** 125,000+ preventable deaths annually in the US alone. $300B+ in wasted healthcare spending globally.

---

## ✅ Our Solution

**MediConnect** is a unified digital health platform that eliminates every barrier between rural patients and quality healthcare through:

1. **AI Symptom Checker** — Describe symptoms in plain language, get instant triage (low/medium/high urgency)
2. **Telemedicine** — Video consultations with verified doctors — no travel required
3. **Smart Appointments** — Book, reschedule, or cancel in seconds with SMS reminders
4. **Digital Health Records** — Complete medical history stored securely, accessible anywhere
5. **Emergency SOS** — One-tap alert sends GPS location to nearest hospital and family
6. **Multi-Language Support** — 12 regional languages with voice input for low-literacy patients
